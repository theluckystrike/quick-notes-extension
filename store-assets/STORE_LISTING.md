# Quick Notes - Chrome Web Store Listing

## Extension Name
Quick Notes - Simple Notepad

## Short Description (132 characters max)
A simple notepad in your browser. Click, type, auto-saves instantly. Works offline. No account needed. 100% private.

## Detailed Description

**Quick Notes** is the simplest way to take notes in your browser. Click the icon, start typing, and your notes are saved automatically. No setup, no account, no cloud - just notes.

### Why Quick Notes?

**Instant Auto-Save**
Every keystroke is saved immediately. No save button, no "Ctrl+S", no data loss. Ever. Close the popup, close the browser - your notes are always there when you come back.

**Works Offline**
No internet connection? No problem. Quick Notes stores everything locally on your device. Your notes are always available, online or offline.

**100% Private**
Your notes never leave your device. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no tracking. What you write stays between you and your browser.

**Quick Insert Tools**
Add timestamps with one click:
- Date button: Insert today's date
- Time button: Insert current time
- Divider: Add a visual separator

**Copy to Clipboard**
One click to copy all your notes. Paste them anywhere - emails, documents, messages.

**Lightweight**
Under 10KB total. Zero performance impact on your browser. No bloat, just notes.

### Perfect For:
- Quick reminders and to-do items
- Meeting notes with timestamps
- Temporary text storage
- Copy-paste workspace
- Jotting down ideas
- Phone numbers and addresses
- Code snippets
- Shopping lists

### Single Purpose Description
This extension provides a simple notepad that saves notes locally in the browser for quick access.

### Permission Justifications

**storage:**
Used to save user's notes locally in the browser so they persist between sessions. Notes are stored on the user's device and never transmitted externally.

**contextMenus:**
Used to add a "Request a feature" option when right-clicking the extension icon, allowing users to quickly access the feedback page. No user data is collected or transmitted.
