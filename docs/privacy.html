Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

Introduction

Quick Notes ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Summary: We collect absolutely nothing. Your notes stay on your device. We have no servers, no analytics, no tracking.

Data Collection

Quick Notes does NOT collect any data whatsoever. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Your notes or any text you type
  • Browsing history or activity
  • Device information
  • IP addresses
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Cookies or tracking data

Local Storage

Your notes are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data:

  • Never leaves your browser
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Is never shared with anyone
  • Stays completely under your control

Permissions Explained

Quick Notes only requires one permission:

  • storage: Used to save your notes locally in your browser. This is the only way your notes persist between sessions.

We do not request access to your browsing data, history, tabs, or any websites.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, analytics, advertising, or tracking tools. There are no external connections made by this extension.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with anyone because we do not collect any data. It's that simple.

Data Security

Your notes are stored securely in your browser's local storage. Only you have access to them. If you uninstall the extension, your notes are deleted along with it.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page or visit our community hub.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is nothing to access, modify, or delete from our end. Your notes are yours alone. You can clear them anytime using the Clear button in the extension, or uninstall the extension to remove all data.
